This Google Analytics report looks at how people are using this site from February 14 to May 13, 2024. Some parts of the site, like the home page, and "Assignments" are really popular, and get a lot of attention. People spend a lot of time there, and they keep coming back. But other part, like "Contact" is not as popular. To make the site better, maybe try making the less popular part more interesting and asking users what they like. By keeping an eye on how much time people spend and how often they visit, the site can be made better for everyone.
